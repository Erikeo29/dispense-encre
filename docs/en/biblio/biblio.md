## Bibliography

### Fundamental Works

1. **Basaran, O. A., Gao, H., & Bhat, P. P.** (2013). *Nonstandard inkjets*. Annual Review of Fluid Mechanics, 45, 85-113.
   - Comprehensive review of non-standard droplet ejection mechanisms.

2. **Derby, B.** (2010). *Inkjet printing of functional and structural materials*. Annual Review of Materials Research, 40, 395-414.
   - Introduction to industrial applications of inkjet printing.

3. **Owens, R. G., & Phillips, T. N.** (2002). *Computational Rheology*. Imperial College Press.
   - Reference for numerical modeling of non-Newtonian fluids.

---

### Numerical Methods

#### VOF (Volume of Fluid)

4. **Hirt, C. W., & Nichols, B. D.** (1981). *Volume of fluid (VOF) method for the dynamics of free boundaries*. Journal of Computational Physics, 39(1), 201-225.
   - Founding paper of the VOF method.

5. **OpenFOAM Foundation** (2023). *OpenFOAM User Guide v11*. [openfoam.org](https://openfoam.org)
   - Official OpenFOAM documentation.

#### FEM / Phase-Field

6. **Jacqmin, D.** (1999). *Calculation of two-phase Navier-Stokes flows using phase-field modeling*. Journal of Computational Physics, 155(1), 96-127.
   - Theoretical foundations of the phase-field model.

7. **FEniCS Project** (2023). *The FEniCS Book*. [fenicsproject.org](https://fenicsproject.org)
   - FEniCS/Firedrake framework documentation.

#### LBM (Lattice Boltzmann)

8. **Succi, S.** (2018). *The Lattice Boltzmann Equation: For Complex States of Flowing Matter*. Oxford University Press.
   - Reference work on the LBM method.

9. **Palabos** (2023). *Palabos User Guide*. [palabos.unige.ch](https://palabos.unige.ch)
   - Palabos library documentation.

#### SPH (Smoothed Particle Hydrodynamics)

10. **Monaghan, J. J.** (2005). *Smoothed particle hydrodynamics*. Reports on Progress in Physics, 68(8), 1703.
    - Comprehensive review of the SPH method.

11. **PySPH** (2023). *PySPH Documentation*. [pysph.readthedocs.io](https://pysph.readthedocs.io)
    - PySPH framework documentation.
